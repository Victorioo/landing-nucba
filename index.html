Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. The band was formed in 2009 by vocalist Tyler Joseph along with Nick Thomas and Chris Salih, who left in 2011. Since their departure, the line-up has consisted of Tyler Joseph and drummer Josh Dun.

Scaled an icy

Scaled And Icy is the sixth full-length album and fourth studio album by American duo Twenty One Pilots released on May 21, 2021 through Fueled by Ramen and Elektra. The album title is an anagram of "Clancy is Dead", a reference to a character from their previous album, Trench.
